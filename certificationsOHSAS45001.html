OHSAS 45001 Consulting

OHSAS 45001 is an Occupation Health and Safety Assessment Series for health and safety management system is intended to help your organization control and minimize occupational health and safety risks. QEMC consultants will assess your manufacturing process and identify key areas lacking in the proper safety protocols. We can assist you not
only with developing and implementing the program, but also long-term assessment and improvement.

Your organization stands to benefit from an OSHAS 45001 certification:

  • Reduced risk through improved safety management of health and safety risks
  • Competitive advantage through a commitment to health and safety
  • Improved performance and operational efficiency through accident management reduction and reduced downtime
  • Reduction of insurance premiums and compensation

Needs information

QEMC consultants will assist with:

  • Planning for hazard identification
  • Risk assessment/control
  • Occupational Health and Safety management, awareness and competence, training, communication, emergency preparedness and response, performance measuring and improvement

OHSAS 45001 Implementation

Our capabilities include more than just generalized assistance in meeting OSHAS 45001 management system requirements. QEMC consultants can provide support related to a wide variety of specific health and safety issues, as needed by your organization:

  • Workers Compensation Management
  • Back to Work Programs
  • Workplace assessments for potential hazards
  • Development of continuous improvement safety processes
  • Audits and follow up assessments to assure continued safety improvement
  • OSHA Compliance and Mitigation Strategies
  • Regulatory Compliance Assistance
  • Required Regulatory Compliance Training
  • Customized Training Program Development and Curriculum Design
  • Developing Job Descriptions
